Learning Plan v2

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

Same answer as previously. My long term goal is to improve my skills as a Product Designer/Developer and to gain the ability to self-publish projects. Ultimately I would like to build my own products.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My strengths remain my curiosity, analytical thought and creativity. After completing Foundations, I think I’m also relatively self aware and can course correct quickly which helps me overcome challenging problems and reevaluate my learning approach. I have a passion for problem solving at every level so even if I’m frustrated, I enjoy the process once I am doing it.

I can also be a terrible procrastinator, and a perfectionist, which makes it worse. During Foundations, I have tried to not over polish some of my work and let “good enough” be enough. I also can feel some background anxiety can cause me to rush through learning exercises and I have to force myself to work more methodically to make sure I am gaining the learning competencies and to make fewer time wasting errors.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

A core component of my lesson plan will be front loading the course work as much as possible to best stay on top of the course work and not put undue time pressure on myself. This will also let me balance my health to the best of my ability.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to work on my leadership skills and also the giving and receiving feedback, which is such an important part of the development process.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

To provide a good learning environment for the cohort and to help cultivate a great culture.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

To keep on top of my course work by front loading work and identifying blockers as early as possible. To review leaning competencies along the way and to note weaknesses in my understanding to address. If I am blocked or struggling, to reach out to member of my cohort or if necessary, a facilitator. If I’m worried about how I am handling the Bootcamp at a personal level I can discuss this with my Human Skills facilitator at our 1:1.
