Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence pertains to the perception, identification, management and expression of emotions. So much of our day to day lives are governed by emotion, our own or others, and a focus on emotional intelligence hopes to better prepare us for a world viewed through this lens.

Where EQ focuses on emotion, IQ is a measure of intelligence across multiple competencies. IQ was once considered the most important trait for success in life, but more recently, the importance of EQ has been found to have just as much, if not greater effect on a persons success. While IQ is still very important, someone with low emotional intelligence might struggle to leverage their IQ to its full potential. As IQ and EQ both play an important role in our success, it is important to foster growth in both areas for best results.
